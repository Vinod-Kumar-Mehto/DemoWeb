Services we offer

Online Survey

We provide access to over 40 online panels and will always be on hand to ensure you have the right mix to meet your online research objectives. All panel management is handled by the Project Manager and your single point of contact will bring in additional panels as needed while maintaining your budget and discretion. We always provide complete transparency to our clients and can work with you to get the most out of your in-house panel and fill in as needed or meet all online sample requirements for every project.

CATI

Telephone surveys are one of the most preferred options that have the potential to maximize response rates and help maintain complete control over the quality of data collected through standardized questionnaires from the sample population. We, at Current Research Aesthetics, are backed by a group of dedicated professionals who are proficient in collecting data in an accurate and complete format. We have a state-of-the-art CATI center located in Kolkata and New Delhi as well as research partners in Jaipur and Dehradun which allow us the flexibility to undertake rapid turnaround research. Current Research Aesthetics adheres to quality standards in conducting cost-effective and accurate telephone surveys in over 40 countries around the world. Providing best-in-class services round the clock, our CATI team efficiently works in multiple shifts and is well versed in a diverse range of market research surveys which include- C-level interviews, consumer surveys, in-depth interviews, B2B Surveys, Market Evaluation Surveys, Opinion Surveys, Brand Image & Awareness Research, Employee/Customer Satisfaction Surveys and many more.

Mixed Mode

Our database is completely phone-recruited and fully profiled to give us layers of targeting in all regions and all countries, as well as information on past partnerships. All respondents chose to be contacted by phone only for the research. They are never emailed survey links or recruited for other types of projects. Strict maintenance of this database and strict protocols allow us to claim a response rate of over 40% and the feasibility to handle your toughest Quant projects.
